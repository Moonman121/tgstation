  • Those with social anxiety may find eye contact to be stressful, and those who foster unhealthy obsessions over other crewmembers may outright staredown their prey.
  • Throwing people really hard into vending machines is not advised if you don't want to clean them up afterwards. Things are considered thrown really hard if: You're throwing them as a hulk, the person you're throwing is a dwarf, and/or if you're throwing someone from a neckgrab
